lumina-sass/color

Automatic Contrast Mixin

The background-color mixin automatically calculates the best text color (light or dark) for readability based on the background color.

@use 'lumina-sass' as *

.auto-contrast-dark
  @include background-color($deep-midnight-blue)

.auto-contrast-light
  @include background-color($soft-white)
I have automatic white text!
I have automatic dark text!
